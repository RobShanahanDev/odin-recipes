Healthy KFC Zinger Burger

Description

Here is a healthy alternative to the famously tasty KFC Zinger Burger!

This page will show you all the ingredients you need and how to make this delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • Two 100g thinly sliced chicken breasts
  • 25g crushed Cornflakes
  • 30g Frank's Red Hot Sauce
  • One slice Low-fat Cheese, halved
  • 10g BBQ Sauce or 10g Low-fat Mayonnaise mixed with 1 Tbsp Sriracha Sauce
  • One Brioche Bun
  • Some Lettuce

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken fillets in Frank's Red Hot Sauce
  2. Coat chicken fillets in crushed Cornflakes
  3. Cook coated chicken fillets in an Air Fryer for 16-18 mins at 180C
  4. Once cooked, place half a slice of Low-fat cheese on each fillet and cook in Air Fryer for an additional 1-2 mins
  5. Add sauce of choice to brioche bun
  6. Add chicken and lettuce to brioche bun
  7. Enjoy!
